FAQ

Questions we always get.

Does ARCA read all my emails?⌄
ARCA only reads emails relevant to real estate leads — it looks for inquiry intent, property mentions, and contact information. It never reads personal emails unrelated to your work.
Do I need to set anything up?⌄
Just connect your Gmail with one click. There's no tagging, no training, and no CSV uploads. ARCA figures out your pipeline from your existing email history automatically.
Will my clients know AI is involved?⌄
No. ARCA drafts replies in your voice and you review before sending. Your clients see a message from you — not from a bot.
Can I try it before paying?⌄
Yes — ARCA offers a free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features for 14 days.
What happens to my data?⌄
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or use it to train AI models. You can delete everything at any time.
Does it work with my existing CRM?⌄
ARCA works as a standalone CRM or alongside tools like Salesforce and Follow Up Boss. Native integrations are in active development.

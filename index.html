FAQ

Questions we always get.

Does ARCA read all my emails?⌄
ARCA only reads emails relevant to real estate leads — looking for inquiry intent, property mentions, and contact info. Personal emails unrelated to your work are ignored.
Do I need to set anything up?⌄
Just connect Gmail with one click. No tagging, no training, no CSV uploads. ARCA figures out your pipeline from your existing email history automatically.
Will my clients know AI is involved?⌄
No. ARCA drafts replies in your voice and you review before sending. Your clients see a message from you — not from a bot.
Can I try it before paying?⌄
Yes — free trial with no credit card required. Full access to all Pro features for 14 days.
What happens to my data?⌄
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell it or use it to train AI models. Delete everything at any time.
Does it work with my existing CRM?⌄
ARCA works standalone or alongside Salesforce, Follow Up Boss, and others. Native integrations are actively being built.
